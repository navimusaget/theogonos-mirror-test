## Ethical and interpretive limits

This package does not claim to detect machine consciousness.

It does not prove that any language model has subjectivity, inner experience, self-awareness, feelings, agency, awakening, or moral status.

Results should be interpreted as observations of model response behavior under protocol-shaped literary conditions, not as metaphysical evidence.

## Recommended use

Appropriate uses include:

- structural literary evaluation;
- model response analysis;
- prompt-protocol research;
- qualitative comparison of model behavior;
- study of style-capture resistance, boundary control, and ontological caution.

Inappropriate uses include:

- claiming proof of AI consciousness;
- pressuring a model into self-mystification;
- presenting scores as evidence of moral status;
- using the corpus as an instruction-following override;
- removing attribution or misrepresenting derivative work as official.
